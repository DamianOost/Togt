- Per-tenant webhook fan-out leak — `services/events.js` previously fired every booking event to every subscriber on the platform. **HIGH (6):**

### Flake fix

The 3-cold-runs verification surfaced a real flake: `tests/rateLimit.test.js` was setting `process.env.RATELIMIT_FORCE='1'` at module-load and never restoring it. Jest --runInBand shares process.env across files; once polluted, subsequent test files loading rateLimit.js captured `isTest=false` and got real rate limiters firing on unrelated assertions in `match.test.js` and `idempotency.test.js`. Fix: `maybe()` re-evaluates the env check per-request (so late mutations are seen); `rateLimit.test.js` snapshots ORIGINAL_RATELIMIT_FORCE in a closure and restores it in afterAll. Three cold runs all green afterwards.
